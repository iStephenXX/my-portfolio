About Moi

"Living life pixel perfect"


Hello one-and-all, I'm Stephen Mwingira. A digital artist and developer, working on cool art designs as well as websites and mobile applications. When you have a project that needs some creative touches, that's where I come in. I'm currently based out of New York City, in the heart of the big apple! There's tons of inspiration and areas--in the city--for me to attain more creative insights to use in my projects. Majority of what I know in this industry is self-taught, and I began learning how to code back in middle school. I'm also very much into engineering, and recently, I took a trip out to the white house to present at the final White House Science Fair under the Obama Administration. You can view the pictures by clicking here if you're interested. FWIW, I was born in Tanzania, but spent a huge chunk of my life so far here in the states, I speak 3 languages, and am a complete sucker for a good ol' chocolate-chip cookie!!
